# Workspace: Onboarding Product Tour

## Context
Introduce a guided onboarding experience for first-time users with dismiss/restore behavior.

## Components
- OnboardingOverlay (frontend)
- UserPrefs (backend)

## Requirements
- Step-by-step tooltips highlighting key areas
- Dismissible with ability to replay
- Track completion and last step
- A/B test different sequences

## Notes
- Ensure accessibility (focus management, escape to dismiss)
- Include lightweight analytics events

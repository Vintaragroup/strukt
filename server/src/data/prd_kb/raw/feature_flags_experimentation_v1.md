# Feature Flags & Experimentation PRD (v1 Raw)

## Overview
Safely ship via feature flags with controlled rollouts and A/B experiments.

## Flag Lifecycle & Targeting
Creation, ownership, TTL; targeting by user/org/segment; kill-switch; cleanup.

## Rollouts & Safety
Gradual rollouts, %, automated rollback on guardrail breaches; freeze rules.

## Experiment Design & Metrics
Hypotheses, metrics, sample size, bucketing, stat-sig; avoid anti-patterns.

## Governance & Audit
Approvals for high-risk flags; audit logs; retention; naming conventions.
